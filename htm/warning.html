Submission Guidelines

Please read the following guidelines carefully to ensure your submission is processed efficiently:

  • All fields marked with an asterisk (*) are mandatory and must be completed.
  • Ensure you have the following documents scanned and ready for upload: Identification Proof, Address Proof, and any relevant Certificates.
  • Double-check your entered information for accuracy to avoid delays.
  • Submissions with incomplete or incorrect information will not be processed.
  • Keep a note of the submission reference number provided after form submission for future inquiries.
  • For assistance, contact our support team available from Monday to Friday, 9:00am - 5:00pm.

Failure to comply with these guidelines may result in an inability to access municipal services. We appreciate your cooperation and understanding.
