Municipality

Welcome to the official website of the Municipality of San Francisco! We are proud to serve our vibrant community with dedication and commitment. Located in the heart of the city, our municipality works tirelessly to improve the quality of life of our residents.

Mission:

The Municipality of San Francisco is committed to providing efficient and transparent services, encouraging citizen participation and maintaining excellence in municipal administration for the benefit of all.

Featured Services:

Online Procedures: We simplify bureaucratic processes with our online procedures system, allowing you to carry out procedures from the comfort of your home. Community Programs: We support a variety of community programs, from cultural events to educational and sports initiatives. Infrastructure Maintenance: We are committed to maintaining our streets, parks and public spaces in optimal conditions for the enjoyment of all.

News and events:

Stay informed about the latest news and events in San Francisco. From local festivals to development projects, here you will find all the information relevant to our community.

Municipal Council:

Meet our local representatives and discover how they are working to make San Francisco a better place to live. Access meeting minutes to stay on top of important decisions

Participates:

We want to hear you. Use our online forms to submit suggestions, complaints or participate in surveys that will help shape future municipal projects and policies.

Thank you for visiting the San Francisco Municipality page. We are here to serve you and together build a prosperous and sustainable future for our community.
